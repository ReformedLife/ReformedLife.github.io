Enter the Vedic fatloss journey

Following the principles of Ayurveda, we delve into our clients' backgrounds and current living situations to understand them better. Our diet and lifestyle plans are tailored to cater to your body type (Prakriti), genetic makeup (Vikruti), and the seasonal influences (Ritucharya) of your residing location.

One of the most remarkable aspects of the course is that it does not rely on any external supplements, making it cost-effective and sustainable even after completing the program.

The 3-Phase Course

We carry out this course in 3 phases.

Adaptation Phase :Adapt your body with healthy eating, daily routines (Dincharya), and 30-minute home workouts for your well-being.

Burner Phase :Expect diet timing changes, fat loss, higher energy, and some visible transformation by the end of stage.

Booster PhasePrepare for muscle building in this stage.
